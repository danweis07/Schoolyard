# Demo Image Credits

The placeholder SVGs in this folder (`logo.svg`, `hero.svg`, `mascot.svg`) are
illustrations created for the Schoolyard demo. They are released under the
project's MIT license and may be reused freely.

For real deployments, replace these with photos of your school. Recommended
sources of free, license-compatible imagery:

- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/s/photos/elementary-school) — Unsplash License
- [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/search/school/) — Pexels License
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/images/search/school/) — Pixabay License

**Important:** Never publish photos of identifiable children without explicit
written consent from their parents or guardians. Photos of buildings,
playgrounds, classrooms (without children), murals, and signage are all safe
defaults for a public-facing school site.

When the Schoolyard reference deployment ships real Unsplash images, this
file will list each photo's source URL and photographer credit per the
Unsplash License attribution guideline.
