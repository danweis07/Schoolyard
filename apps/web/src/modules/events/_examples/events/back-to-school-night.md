Welcome back! Join us for an evening to reconnect with teachers and fellow families at the start of the school year.

## Schedule

- **6:00 PM** — Welcome in the main hall
- **6:15 PM** — Visit your child's classroom
- **7:15 PM** — PTA meet-and-greet and refreshments
- **7:45 PM** — Closing remarks

## Who should come

Parents, guardians, and students of all grades. Younger siblings welcome.

## Accessibility

The main hall is wheelchair accessible. Interpretation available on request — contact the school office one week in advance.
