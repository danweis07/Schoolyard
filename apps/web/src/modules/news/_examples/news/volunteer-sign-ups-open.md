Every minute a parent volunteers goes straight back into classrooms. This year we need help with:

- **Event shifts** — one-to-three hour slots at school events throughout the year
- **Classroom helpers** — weekly or monthly reading, art, or project support
- **Committee members** — year-long roles on fundraising, events, or communications

No experience needed — just a willingness to help. Visit the Volunteer page to see current openings.
