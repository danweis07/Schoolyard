The Fall Carnival is Longfellow's signature community celebration. Last year we raised over $12,000 for classroom enrichment and welcomed more than 500 families.

## What to expect

- **Games & activities** — pumpkin bowling, ring toss, face painting, balloon animals, bouncy castles, and the ever-popular cake walk
- **Food trucks** — three local vendors confirmed: Tacos El Pastor, Mr. Bing Dumplings, and Cool Tots Ice Cream
- **Live music** — student performances from grades 3-5 plus a guest set from a parent jazz quartet
- **Silent auction** — donated experiences, gift baskets, and classroom-made art

## How to help

We need 60+ volunteer shifts. Sign up via the Volunteer page or email Maria Chen at volunteer@longfellowsfpta.org. Donations of new prizes and gently-used books welcome at the office through October 15.
