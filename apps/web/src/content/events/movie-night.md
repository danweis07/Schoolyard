Family Movie Night is a beloved Longfellow tradition. This month we're screening **Coco** (2017, rated PG, runtime 105 min, available with English audio and Spanish subtitles).

Doors open at 6:30 PM. Bring blankets, pillows, and your reusable water bottles. The PTA sells popcorn ($2), juice boxes ($1), and hot dog dinners ($5) — proceeds support next month's events.

This is a free event. All families welcome.
