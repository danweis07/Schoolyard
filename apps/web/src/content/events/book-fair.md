The Longfellow Spring Book Fair runs Monday through Friday, May 12-16. Hundreds of titles for K-5 readers, with bilingual selections in Spanish and Chinese.

## Hours

- **Mon-Fri** 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM during school hours
- **Wednesday** open until 7:00 PM for working parents

## Wishlist program

If your family can't afford a book this week, leave a note at the front table. Donor families have already pre-funded scholarships so every Longfellow student can take home at least one book.

## Volunteer

We need 20 volunteer shifts across the week to staff the cash register and help kids find titles. Sign up via the Volunteer page.
