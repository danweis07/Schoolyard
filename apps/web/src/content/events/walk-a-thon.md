The Walk-a-Thon is Longfellow's biggest single-day fundraiser. Every dollar raised stays at our school and funds programs the district doesn't pay for: classroom art supplies, music instruments, science kits, and field trip scholarships.

## How it works

1. Students bring home pledge sheets in mid-April
2. Family and neighbors sponsor each lap (or a flat amount)
3. On event day, students walk for 60 minutes — no minimum laps required
4. Pledges are collected the following week

Last year 280 students participated and we raised $32,000.

## Volunteers needed

We need parents to staff water stations, count laps, and supervise route safety. Sign up via the Volunteer page.
