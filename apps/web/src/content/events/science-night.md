Our annual Family Science Night turns the school into a giant lab. 15 hands-on experiment stations, run by Longfellow 5th graders and parent volunteers from local biotech companies.

## What's happening

- **Mobile planetarium** — SFUSD's inflatable dome, 20-minute shows running every 30 minutes
- **Slime making, oobleck, and polymer experiments**
- **Bridge building** with toothpicks and gumdrops
- **Solar oven s'mores** (weather permitting)
- **Microscope station** featuring pond water and household items

Free admission. Walk-up registration at the door, but pre-register on the volunteer page if you can — it helps us plan supplies.
