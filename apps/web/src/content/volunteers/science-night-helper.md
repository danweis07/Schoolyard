Family Science Night is our biggest hands-on academic event of the year. Each of the 15 experiment stations is run by a Longfellow 5th grader paired with a parent volunteer (that's you).

You don't need a science background — the 5th graders have practiced their experiments and can explain the science. You're there to keep the station tidy, answer parent questions, and supervise younger kids.

Stations include slime making, oobleck, bridge building, microscopes, solar ovens, and more. We'll match you to one when you sign up.
