The Fall Carnival doesn't happen by magic. We need a small dedicated team that can commit to 2-3 weekend prep sessions in October leading up to the event.

Tasks include:

- Sorting and pricing donated prizes
- Assembling game station signage and equipment
- Building the cake walk numbered floor
- Pre-bagging cotton candy and popcorn for sale
- Creating decorations with our 5th grade art students

This is the role for parents who want an in-depth way to contribute and meet other involved families. You'll get to know the PTA leadership and have a major hand in the day-of success of our biggest event.
