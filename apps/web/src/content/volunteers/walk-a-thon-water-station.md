The Walk-a-Thon water station is the perfect first-time volunteer gig. We need 6 parents to staff two water tables along the route at Crocker Amazon Park. You'll hand out cups of water, refill jugs, and cheer on the kids as they walk past.

**What you'll need:** comfortable shoes, sun protection, and a smile.

**What we provide:** all supplies, tables, water, cups, and a parent volunteer t-shirt.
