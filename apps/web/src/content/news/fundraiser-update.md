Thanks to 142 contributing families, the Longfellow PTA has raised **$28,750** toward our $45,000 annual fund goal — that's 64% of our target with three months to go.

## Where the money has gone

Through February, we've spent $19,200 on:

- **$8,400** — Classroom supplies and books (every K-5 teacher received a $300 spring stipend)
- **$4,200** — Music program (instrument repair, sheet music, guest workshops)
- **$3,100** — Field trip scholarships (24 families, full coverage)
- **$2,000** — Outdoor classroom seed funding (planters, garden tools, soil)
- **$1,500** — School-wide art supplies and project materials

## What we still need

To finish the year strong we need to raise the remaining **$16,250** by June. That money funds:

- The 5th grade promotion celebration
- Summer learning kits for families who want them
- Initial deposits on next year's traveling theater visit
- Scholarship coverage for next year's after-school enrichment

## How to give

Every dollar makes a difference. Visit our [Donate page](/donate) — you can give a one-time amount or set up a small monthly gift. PayPal, Venmo, and check are all accepted. The PTA is a registered 501(c)(3) so your gift is tax-deductible.

Many employers will match your gift dollar-for-dollar. Email me at treasurer@longfellowsfpta.org if you'd like help with the matching paperwork.

Thank you for keeping Longfellow strong.
