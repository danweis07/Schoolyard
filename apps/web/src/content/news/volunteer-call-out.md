Spring is the busiest season at Longfellow. Between the Walk-a-Thon, Family Movie Night, Book Fair, Science Night, and our 5th grade promotion, we're going to need every family to pitch in at least once.

## Why we need you

Last year 38% of Longfellow families volunteered for at least one event. We'd love to push that to 60% this year. Even a single 90-minute shift makes a huge difference — and it's the easiest way to meet other parents.

## Roles open right now

- **Walk-a-Thon water station** — May 2, 90 minutes, easy for first-timers
- **Movie Night setup crew** — April 25, 5:30-6:30 PM
- **Book Fair cashier** — May 12-16, 1-hour shifts
- **Science Night experiment helper** — April 30, 5:30-8:30 PM
- **Carnival prep team** — October, multiple shifts

Visit the [Volunteer page](/volunteer) to sign up, or email me at volunteer@longfellowsfpta.org with any questions.

Thank you for everything you already do for our school.
