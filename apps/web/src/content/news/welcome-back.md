Welcome back, Longfellow families! It's hard to believe summer is over and we're ready to begin the 2026-27 school year. Our staff has been working hard all August to make sure your children walk into a warm, ready, and inspiring classroom on Day One.

## New faces

We're thrilled to welcome three new staff members to the Longfellow team:

- **Ms. Rivera** joins us in 2nd grade, coming from Jefferson Elementary
- **Mr. Patel** joins our music program, replacing the much-loved Mr. Kim who retired in June
- **Ms. Wong** joins our front office team — please say hello when you stop by

## What's new

This year we're piloting a new outdoor classroom in the schoolyard garden, made possible by last year's Walk-a-Thon proceeds. Every grade will have at least one weekly lesson outdoors.

We're also expanding our after-school enrichment program with new offerings in chess, ceramics, and robotics. Sign-ups go live August 25.

## Stay connected

The PTA is hosting a Welcome Back coffee on the first Friday of school. Bring your questions, meet other families, and learn how you can get involved.

Looking forward to a wonderful year together.
