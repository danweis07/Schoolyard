Thank you to everyone who voted in this year's PTA board election. We had record turnout — 187 families cast ballots, up from 130 last year.

## Your 2026-27 PTA Board

- **Sarah Kim** — President
- **James Okonkwo** — Vice President
- **David Nguyen** — Treasurer (continuing)
- **Priya Patel** — Secretary
- **Maria Chen** — Volunteer Coordinator (continuing)
- **Lina Rodriguez** — Communications Chair

The new board takes office July 1. They'll be hosting a transition meeting in early June and will introduce themselves at the first all-school assembly in August.

## Thank you to our outgoing officers

Special thanks to outgoing President Tom Reilly, who served two terms and led us through our biggest fundraising year ever, and to Communications Chair Lisa Park who built the new school newsletter from scratch.

If you're interested in getting more involved next year — committee chairs, room parents, event leads — please reach out to Sarah Kim at president@longfellowsfpta.org. There's always room for more.
