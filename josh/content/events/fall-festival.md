The Fall Festival is FSK's signature back-to-school celebration. It's the perfect time to meet new families, reconnect with old friends, and learn about volunteer opportunities for the year ahead.

## Activities

- **Kids' games** — face painting, balloon artists, relay races, art stations
- **Food vendors** — neighborhood favorites from the Sunset District
- **Live performances** — student talent showcase and parent band
- **Annual Fund kickoff** — learn where your donations go and how to set up employer matching
- **Volunteer sign-ups** — browse open roles and sign up on the spot

## New this year

We're adding a **multicultural food fair** where families can share dishes from their home cultures. Sign up to contribute a dish by September 15.
