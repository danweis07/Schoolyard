The Spring Gala is FSK's premier fundraising event, bringing together parents, teachers, alumni, and community supporters for an unforgettable evening.

## What to expect

- **Cocktail reception** — 6:00 PM with hosted bar and appetizers
- **Dinner** — catered sit-down dinner
- **Live auction** — unique experiences including teacher adventures, VIP sports packages, vacation getaways, and classroom art projects
- **Silent auction** — browse 50+ donated items via mobile bidding
- **Fund-a-Need** — direct appeals for specific programs (STEAM equipment, garden supplies, library books)
- **Live music and dancing** — celebrate with the FSK community

## Auction donations

We're seeking auction items! Contact David Okafor at pta_board@francisscottkeyschool.org to donate experiences, goods, or services. All donations are tax-deductible.

## Tickets

Individual tickets and table sponsorships available. Scholarship tickets available for families who need them — no one is excluded.

## Our goal: $100,000

Last year we raised $95,000. This year, we're aiming for $100,000 to fully fund STEAM residencies, the garden program, lunch and recess supports, new turf maintenance, and classroom books and supplies.
