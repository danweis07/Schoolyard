Passport Day is FSK's signature multicultural celebration where our diverse community shares cultures from around the world.

## How it works

- Each classroom adopts a country and creates an interactive exhibit
- Students and families visit each "country" and get their passport stamped
- Cultural displays include food samples, music, crafts, and information
- Students who visit every country earn a special prize

## Countries represented (2025-2026)

Past countries have included China, Mexico, Philippines, Russia, Ireland, India, Japan, Ethiopia, Brazil, Italy, Ghana, and Vietnam — reflecting the real heritage of FSK families.

## Get involved

- Volunteer to help your child's classroom prepare their country exhibit
- Donate cultural items, books, or music for the displays
- Share your family's heritage by presenting at your child's classroom station
