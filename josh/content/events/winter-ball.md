The Winter Ball is a magical evening where students get to dress up, dance with their families, and celebrate the holiday season together.

## Highlights

- **DJ and dance floor** — age-appropriate music for all grades
- **Photo station** — holiday-themed backdrop for family photos
- **Refreshments** — cookies, hot chocolate, and apple cider
- **Craft corner** — make ornaments and holiday cards

## Admission

Free for all FSK families. Donations to the Annual Fund gratefully accepted at the door.
