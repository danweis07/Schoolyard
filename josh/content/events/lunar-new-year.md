FSK's Lunar New Year Festival is one of the highlights of our school year, reflecting the rich cultural diversity of our Sunset District community.

## Performances

- **Lion dance** — professional troupe performance in the schoolyard
- **Student performances** — songs, dances, and poetry in Mandarin, Cantonese, and Vietnamese
- **Calligraphy demonstrations** — try your hand at writing New Year greetings

## Activities

- **Craft stations** — make paper lanterns, red envelopes, and lucky knot bracelets
- **Cultural exhibits** — learn about Lunar New Year traditions from different countries
- **Photo booth** — with traditional costumes and props

## Food

Parent volunteers prepare a multicultural feast featuring dumplings, spring rolls, rice cakes, and other traditional New Year dishes. Free for all families.

## Volunteer

We need parent volunteers to help with food prep (morning of), craft stations, and cleanup. Mandarin, Cantonese, and Vietnamese speakers especially needed to help with cultural exhibit stations.
