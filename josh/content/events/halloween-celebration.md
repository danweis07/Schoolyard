One of the most fun days of the year! Students parade in their costumes through the schoolyard while families cheer from the sidelines.

## Schedule

- **1:00 PM** — Students line up by class
- **1:15 PM** — Parade begins (K-5 in order)
- **1:45 PM** — Classroom celebrations begin
- **2:30 PM** — Regular dismissal

## Parent volunteers

Each classroom needs 2-3 parent helpers for the classroom celebration. Sign up with your child's teacher directly.
