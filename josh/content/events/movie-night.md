Family Movie Night is one of our most beloved traditions. The movie will be voted on by students the week before — family-friendly options only!

## What to bring

- Blankets and pillows
- Your family and friends (non-FSK families welcome!)

## What we provide

- Big screen and sound system
- Popcorn ($2/bag), pizza ($3/slice), juice boxes ($1)
- All proceeds support the PTA general fund

## Volunteers needed

We need 6 volunteers for setup (4:30 PM) and cleanup (8:30 PM). Sign up on the Volunteer page.
