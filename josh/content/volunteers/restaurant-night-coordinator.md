Restaurant nights are one of our easiest fundraisers — families eat at a local restaurant and a portion of sales goes to the PTA. We need 2-3 coordinators to:

- **Build relationships** with Sunset District restaurants willing to partner
- **Schedule events** — one per month, rotating restaurants
- **Promote** — create flyers, post on social media, send reminders via newsletter
- **Track proceeds** — follow up with restaurants to collect PTA's portion

**Great for:** parents who love food, know the neighborhood, and want a low-key but impactful role.

**Past partners:** Outerlands, Devil's Teeth Baking Company, Thanh Long, Hook Fish Co, and more.
