The Spring Gala is FSK's biggest fundraiser, raising $95,000+ last year. We need a team of 10-15 parents to make it happen. Roles include:

- **Auction item solicitation** — reach out to local businesses for donated items and experiences
- **Catalog & display** — photograph, describe, and organize auction items
- **Venue setup** — help with decorations and logistics on the day of the event
- **Night-of support** — manage check-in, bidding, and checkout

No experience needed — just enthusiasm and reliability. This is a great way to make a big impact while working on a fun project with other parents.
