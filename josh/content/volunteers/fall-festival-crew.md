Our Fall Festival needs 40+ volunteers to run smoothly. We offer flexible half-day shifts:

- **Morning setup** (9:00 AM – 11:00 AM) — set up tables, hang decorations, arrange game stations
- **Activity stations** (11:00 AM – 2:00 PM or 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM) — run a game booth, face painting, or food station
- **Afternoon cleanup** (3:00 PM – 4:00 PM) — break down and clean up the schoolyard

**What we provide:** volunteer t-shirt, free food and drinks, and the satisfaction of building community.
