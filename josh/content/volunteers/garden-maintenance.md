The FSK garden program serves every grade with weekly hands-on science and nutrition lessons. We need parent volunteers to help maintain the garden and assist during classes.

**Weekly tasks:**
- Water and weed garden beds
- Help students with planting and harvesting
- Assist the garden instructor during class visits
- Occasional weekend work parties for big projects (building raised beds, composting)

**No experience needed!** Our garden instructor will show you everything. This is a wonderful way to connect with students and spend time outdoors.
