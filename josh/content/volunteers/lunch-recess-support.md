Our lunch and recess program benefits hugely from parent presence. Volunteers help by:

- Setting up organized games (four square, jump rope, soccer)
- Reading aloud in the library during indoor recess
- Helping students open tricky lunch containers (it matters more than you think!)
- Being a friendly face on the yard

**This is the most flexible volunteer role we offer** — sign up for any day that works for you, as often or as rarely as fits your schedule. Even once a month makes a difference.
