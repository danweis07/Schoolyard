Thanks to the incredible generosity of FSK families, we're expanding enrichment programming this year. Here's what your Annual Fund and Gala donations are making possible:

## STEAM residencies

Every grade will have a 6-week STEAM residency this year, up from 4 weeks last year. Topics include robotics (K-1), coding (2-3), and engineering design challenges (4-5). Provided in partnership with SF STEM.

## Garden program

The FSK garden is thriving! Students visit weekly for hands-on lessons in biology, nutrition, and environmental science. New this year: a composting station and a "seed to table" cooking series where students prepare food they've grown.

## After-school activities

Sign-ups open September 20 for fall session:

- Chess club (Tue/Thu)
- Art studio (Mon/Wed)
- Coding club (Tue)
- Running club (Wed/Fri)
- Drama (Mon/Thu)
- Mandarin enrichment (Fri)

All activities are subsidized by the PTA. Scholarship spots available for every program.

## How to support

The best way to support enrichment is through the Annual Fund. Even $25 makes a difference. Visit the Donate page to contribute.
