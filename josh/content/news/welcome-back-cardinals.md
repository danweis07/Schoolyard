Welcome back, FSK families! We are thrilled to open our doors for another incredible school year. Our dedicated staff has been preparing all summer to create warm, engaging classrooms for your children.

## New staff members

We're excited to welcome several new team members:

- **Ms. Nakamura** joins our 1st grade team, bringing experience from Sunset Elementary
- **Mr. Dominguez** is our new music teacher, a professional musician and educator
- **Ms. Petrov** joins the after-school program as enrichment coordinator

## What's new this year

- **Expanded STEAM program** — thanks to PTA funding, every grade now has weekly STEAM sessions
- **Garden classroom** — our renovated garden space is ready for hands-on science and nutrition lessons
- **New turf** — the schoolyard turf has been replaced, funded by last year's Gala
- **Morning mindfulness** — a new 5-minute daily practice in every classroom

## Important dates

- **August 20** — First day of school
- **August 28** — Back to School Night
- **September 10** — PTA General Meeting (6:30 PM, library)
- **September 26** — Fall Festival

Looking forward to a wonderful year together!
