What an incredible night! The FSK community came together at the Great American Music Hall for our annual Spring Gala and raised $95,000 — the most in school history.

## Where the money goes

Every dollar raised stays at FSK. Here's the breakdown:

- **$30,000** — STEAM residencies and equipment for all grades
- **$20,000** — Garden program (supplies, instructor, raised beds)
- **$15,000** — Lunch and recess enrichment (games, equipment, yard supervisors)
- **$15,000** — Classroom books and supplies beyond district allocation
- **$10,000** — Turf maintenance and schoolyard improvements
- **$5,000** — Family support fund (field trip scholarships, school supplies for families in need)

## Thank you to our sponsors

A special thank you to the 12 local businesses that sponsored tables, the 45 families that donated auction items, and the 30 parent volunteers who made the night possible.

## Looking ahead

Next year's goal is $100,000. If your employer matches charitable gifts, that's the easiest way to double your impact. Contact Anna Chen for matching gift forms.
