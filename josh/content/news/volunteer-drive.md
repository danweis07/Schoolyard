FSK runs on parent power. Last year, 180 families contributed over 4,000 volunteer hours. This year, we want to reach 200 families — and we've made it easier than ever to get involved.

## New this year: Flexible volunteering

Not everyone can take a full day off work. That's why we've created three tiers:

- **Drop-in shifts** (1-2 hours) — event setup, lunchtime reading, classroom help
- **Project-based roles** (5-10 hours/semester) — committee work, event planning, grant writing
- **Ongoing commitments** (weekly/monthly) — garden maintenance, library shelving, lunch duty

## How to sign up

Visit the Volunteer page on this site to browse open roles and sign up online. You can also stop by the volunteer table at the Fall Festival on September 26.

## Background checks

All regular volunteers need a current background check through SFUSD. It's free and takes about 2 weeks. Start the process early so you're ready when opportunities come up.
